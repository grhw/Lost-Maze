Lost Maze

How to play:
  • You start at a random point in the map.
  • Figure out where you are by typing look. This tells you the cells to your left and right.
  • Type left or right to rotate.
  • Type move to move forward.
  • Get to the edge of the maze to win.
  • Black = walls.
  • If you give up, you can type end to show where you were on the map.
  • You can also click anywhere on the map to mark a cell.
  • Type any number to change the maze size.
  • Type invert to invert colors.
